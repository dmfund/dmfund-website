What We Look For

Investment Criteria

We invest in search funds and directly acquire businesses that meet our core criteria. We seek enduring businesses with defensible positions and strong cash flow characteristics.

$3-15M

Revenue Range

We target companies with $3M to $15M in annual revenue, providing a solid foundation for growth across each of our investment strategies.

20%+

EBITDA Margins

Strong profitability is essential. We look for businesses with healthy margins that demonstrate pricing power and operational efficiency.

90%+

Customer Retention

Recurring revenue, long-term contracts, and sticky customer relationships are hallmarks of the businesses we seek to acquire and steward.
