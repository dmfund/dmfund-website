Our Approach

How We Partner with Entrepreneurs

We believe the best businesses are built by operators with deep industry knowledge, a bias for action, and the drive to lead. We provide the capital, mentorship, and long-term commitment they need.

01

Search & Acquisition

We back talented entrepreneurs in their search for the right business to acquire. From refining the thesis to closing the deal, we are a hands-on partner at every stage.

02

Operational Partnership

Post-acquisition, we work alongside our CEOs as engaged board members and strategic advisors. We bring pattern recognition from across our portfolio without imposing a playbook.

03

Long-Term Stewardship

We invest with a permanent ownership mindset. Our family capital allows us to hold businesses indefinitely, aligning our incentives with sustainable growth and community impact.

Our Story

Built on Relationships,
Driven by Purpose

Davis Macleod was founded on the belief that the search fund model represents one of the most compelling paths to entrepreneurship and value creation in the lower middle market.

As a family investment office, we bring more than capital to the table. We bring decades of operational experience, a network of trusted advisors and operators, and the patience that comes from investing our own money alongside our partners.
